Who we are

We believe every celebration is better with a wagging tail.

There’s just something about puppies — the joy they bring, the way they make strangers smile, the way they turn an ordinary gathering into something unforgettable. Add a pup to any event, and suddenly it feels lighter, happier, and a little more magical.

Our inspiration behind it all is Cookie — our sweet boy who never misses an opportunity for a treat or a little extra love. There isn’t a single time we walk past his favorite coffee shop or local store that he doesn’t excitedly pull us toward the door, hoping for a snack and a pet from whoever’s inside. Watching his pure joy in those tiny moments is what sparked this idea.

We realized something simple: dogs don’t just attend events — they elevate them. They create connection. They bring laughter. They remind us to celebrate the little things.

So we created a paw-ty cart that does exactly that — brings treats, play, and joy together in one unforgettable experience.

Because the best memories usually have a little fur on them. 🐾✨
